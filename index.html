About Me

I am a current senior studying computer science with a concentration in software development at Worcester State University. Although I hope to graduate with a Computer Science degree, I did not start my college career as a Computer Science student. In fact, I majored in Biology with a concentration in pre-dental at Bridgewater State University (2012). In the summer of 2014 I watched a very inspirational video of a 14 year old programmer who dreams in code. Ever since that video, I decided to change my major and transfer to Worcester State University (2014) to finish my college career. Now as a senior, I find that programming has become more than just something I do in school. I enjoy reading articles about testing, listening to programming podcasts, working on projects and most of all, learning new languages.

When I'm not busy programming, you'll probably find me at the gym, playing sports (tennis, basketball & football), binge watching t.v shows on Netflix (The Office) reading or eating.
